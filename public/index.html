We feed fresh because we believe better food for dogs is the
key to a long, healthy life. That’s why we encourage
everyone to pay attention to their dogs' diet.

Our dogs' digestive systems were made for natural, fresh meat
and vegetables, not highly processed food from a bag. In fact,
simply adding fresh food to dogs' diets has helped them
recover from serious illnesses, improved skin conditions and
allergies, and resulted in more balanced energy levels.
